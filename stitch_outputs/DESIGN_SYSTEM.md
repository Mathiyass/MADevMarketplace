# MA_Dev Marketplace - Design System

## Typography
- **Headings/Code/Numbers**: JetBrains Mono Bold
- **Body/Labels**: Inter Regular / Medium

## Color Palette
- `--bg`: `#0D1117`
- `--surface`: `#161B22`
- `--surface-2`: `#21262D`
- `--border`: `#30363D`
- `--text`: `#E6EDF3`
- `--muted`: `#8B949E`
- `--accent`: `#00E5CC`
- `--success`: `#3FB950`
- `--danger`: `#F85149`
- `--warning`: `#D29922`

## Category Colors
- **Local AI**: `#FF6B6B`
- **Scripts**: `#4ECDC4`
- **Linux ISO**: `#45B7D1`
- **Tools**: `#FFA07A`

## Components
- **Cards**: 8dp radius, 1dp border `#30363D`, NO shadows, flat design
- **Buttons**: 56dp height, 8dp radius, teal fill (`#00E5CC`) or teal outline
- **Inputs**: 52dp height, `#161B22` fill, `#30363D` border, 8dp radius, `#8B949E` placeholder
